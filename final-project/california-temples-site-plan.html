Exploring LDS Temples Across California

The title was chosen to clearly reflect the purpose of this site and to help visitors quickly understand the type of information they can expect to find here.

Purpose

The goal of this site is to serve as a reliable source of information about LDS temples located throughout the state of California. It provides a straightforward and recognizable description, helping visitors immediately understand the focus of the site—temples, their locations, and related insights—all within the context of the Church in California
